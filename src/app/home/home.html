3. What kind of designs doesn’t Designpro offer? ▾

To maintain quality and speed, we don’t support:

  • Extremely complex or fine-art illustrations
  • Fillable/interactive PDFs
  • Complex photo manipulation
  • Digital painting & game assets
  • 3D/CAD projects
  • HTML, CSS, UI/UX design
  • Programming/coding/development
  • Unsupported file formats
  • Trademarked names, brands, or quotes
  • Content writing/manual typing
  • Inappropriate or offensive requests
4. What details should my design request include? ▾

We ask for input across three simple areas:

  1. Describe the design you need.
  2. Select your brand profile.
  3. Attach any assets, images, and copy needed.

Our guided, AI-powered smart request form (inside the Designpro Platform) lets you set dimensions & file types, upload files, and even search stock images. We also recommend creating Brand Profiles (logos, colors, fonts, images) so designers stay on-brand. Create unlimited Brand Profiles for multiple brands.
